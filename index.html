About Me

Chongjie Ye is a Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, supervised by Prof. Xiaoguang Han. He received his bachelor's degree from CUHK-Shenzhen in 2022. His research focuses on generative 3D geometry modeling, with particular emphasis on photometric stereo, surface normal estimation, and 3D reconstruction from single or multiple images.
